## 1. Chủ đề

**"Measuring AI Agent Autonomy in Practice"** — Anthropic Societal Impacts team (Feb 18, 2026)

Anthropic vừa công bố nghiên cứu THỰC TẾ đầu tiên về cách con người sử dụng AI agents "in the wild" — phân tích TRIỆU conversations từ Claude Code và Public API.

**Vấn đề nghiên cứu giải quyết:**
- Chúng ta KHÔNG BIẾT agents được dùng như thế nào ngoài đời thực
- Pre-deployment evaluations (như METR benchmarks) chỉ đo khả năng, KHÔNG đo hành vi thực tế
- Không có chuẩn đo "autonomy" — agents chạy độc lập bao lâu? Trong ngành nào? Rủi ro gì?
- Post-deployment monitoring infrastructure CHƯA TỒN TẠI ở hầu hết platforms

**Đây là research QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT** cho CommandMate vì:
- Anthropic KHÔNG chỉ build tools — họ đo đạc CÁCH NGƯỜI DÙNG THỰC TẾ tương tác với agents
- Data-driven insights → không phải speculation
- Họ đã build infrastructure để track autonomy, risk, human oversight AT SCALE

## 2. Tóm tắt: Key Findings từ Anthropic

### A. Claude Code đang chạy tự động NGÀY CÀNG LÂU HƠN

**Finding 1: Turn duration tăng gấp đôi trong 3 tháng**
- **99.9th percentile turn duration**: <25 phút (tháng 10/2025) → >45 phút (tháng 1/2026)
- Median vẫn ổn định (~45 giây) — chỉ có LONG TAIL mới tăng
- Tăng SMOOTH qua các model releases → KHÔNG PHẢI do model giỏi hơn, mà do:
  - Users build trust over time
  - Users give agents more ambitious tasks
  - Product itself improves (daily updates)

**Finding 2: Deployment overhang**
- METR eval: Claude Opus 4.5 có thể làm tasks 5 giờ (50% success rate)
- Thực tế: 99.9th percentile chỉ 42 phút
- → **Agents CÓ THỂ làm nhiều hơn những gì users CHO PHÉP**

**Insight cho CommandMate:**
> "The autonomy models are capable of handling exceeds what they exercise in practice."

CommandMate cần design cho **tương lai gần**, không chỉ hiện tại. Nếu users cho phép agents chạy 45+ phút TODAY → 6 tháng nữa sẽ là BAO LÂU?

---

### B. Experienced users GIÁM SÁT KHÁC với new users

**Finding 3: Auto-approve tăng theo experience**
- New users (<50 sessions): ~20% dùng full auto-approve
- Experienced users (750+ sessions): >40% dùng full auto-approve
- → Users learn to TRUST agents over time

**Finding 4: Interrupt rate TĂNG theo experience (counterintuitive!)**
- New users: interrupt 5% turns
- Experienced users: interrupt 9% turns
- → **Experienced users shift từ "approve từng step" sang "monitor + intervene khi cần"**

**Pattern:** Auto-approve tăng + Interrupt tăng = **Active monitoring, not abdication**

**Insight cho CommandMate:**
> "Effective oversight doesn't require approving every action but being in a position to intervene when it matters."

CommandMate KHÔNG NÊN force Sếp approve từng action. Thay vào đó:
- Default: Agent chạy tự động
- Sếp có thể "pause" bất cứ lúc nào
- Highlight CÁC BƯỚC QUAN TRỌNG để Sếp review (e.g., trước khi send email, deploy code)

---

### C. Claude tự PAUSE để hỏi — nhiều hơn humans interrupt

**Finding 5: Agent-initiated stops > human-initiated stops**
- Tasks đơn giản: Claude hỏi clarification ~10%
- Tasks phức tạp: Claude hỏi clarification ~24%
- Human interrupt: tasks phức tạp chỉ ~12%
- → **Claude tự GIỚI HẠN autonomy khi uncertain**

**Table: Tại sao Claude dừng vs Tại sao humans interrupt**

| Claude tự dừng | Humans interrupt |
|---------------|------------------|
| Present user với choice giữa approaches (35%) | Cung cấp missing technical context (32%) |
| Gather diagnostic info / test results (21%) | Claude slow/hanging/excessive (17%) |
| Clarify vague requests (13%) | Got enough help, proceed independently (7%) |
| Request credentials/tokens (12%) | Want to take next step manually (7%) |
| Get approval trước khi action (11%) | Change requirements mid-task (5%) |

**Insight cho CommandMate:**
> "Training models to recognize their own uncertainty is an important safety property."

CommandMate agents NÊN:
- Tự pause khi không chắc chắn (KHÔNG chỉ wait for human interrupt)
- Hiện confidence score cho từng step
- Suggest checkpoints: "I'm 85% confident — want to review before I proceed?"

---

### D. Agents được dùng trong HIGH-RISK domains, nhưng CHƯA AT SCALE

**Finding 6: Risk distribution**
- 80% tool calls có safeguards (restricted permissions, human approval)
- 73% có human in the loop
- **Only 0.8% actions are irreversible** (e.g., send email to customer)
- → Majority = low-risk, reversible

**Finding 7: Domain distribution**
- **Software engineering: ~50% of tool calls**
- Business intelligence, customer service, sales, finance, e-commerce: mỗi cái <5%
- **Emerging**: Healthcare, cybersecurity (nhưng still very small)

**Finding 8: Risk vs Autonomy scatter plot (Figure 5)**
- Most clusters = low-risk, low-medium autonomy
- **Upper-right quadrant (high risk + high autonomy) is SPARSE but NOT EMPTY**
- Examples of high-risk clusters:
  - Implement API key exfiltration backdoors (risk: 6.0, autonomy: 8.0)
  - Retrieve patient medical records (risk: 4.4, autonomy: 3.2)
  - Deploy bug fixes to production (risk: 3.6, autonomy: 4.8)

**Caveat:** Many high-risk actions là **evaluations/red-teaming**, KHÔNG phải production.

**Insight cho CommandMate:**
> "We are in the early days of agent adoption. The frontier of risk and autonomy will expand."

CommandMate cần **risk scoring framework** NGAY BÂY GIỜ, trước khi high-risk tasks scale up.

## 3. Liên quan CommandMate

### Trụ 1: Outcome-based Pricing

**Vấn đề:** Làm sao Sếp biết outcome "có chất lượng" để approve payment?

**Anthropic insight: "Autonomy is co-constructed by model, user, and product"**

Outcome quality KHÔNG CHỈ do agent — còn do:
- **Model behavior** (Claude tự pause khi uncertain)
- **User oversight** (Sếp có intervene không? Có trust agent không?)
- **Product design** (CommandMate có highlight risk points không?)

**Ứng dụng cho CommandMate:**

1. **Quality = f(autonomy, risk, oversight)**
   - Outcome với HIGH autonomy + LOW oversight → CẦN higher quality bar
   - Outcome với LOW autonomy + HIGH oversight → có thể lenient hơn
   - Pricing điều chỉnh theo risk-autonomy profile:
     - Low-risk, low-autonomy task (e.g., schedule meeting) → flat fee
     - High-risk, high-autonomy task (e.g., deploy production code) → outcome-based + quality threshold

2. **Track user trust over time** (học từ Anthropic auto-approve data)
   - Sếp mới: CommandMate recommend manual review
   - Sếp experienced: CommandMate enable auto-approve cho low-risk tasks
   - Dashboard: "Bạn đã auto-approve 85% tasks trong 30 ngày qua — agent X đáng tin cậy"

3. **Agent confidence score** (học từ Claude self-pause behavior)
   - Agent submit outcome + confidence score (0-100%)
   - Nếu confidence <80% → flag cho Sếp review
   - Nếu confidence ≥90% + low-risk → auto-approve (nếu Sếp đã enable)
   - Pricing: High-confidence outcomes charge full, low-confidence charge partial (hoặc refund nếu fail)

---

### Trụ 2: Tools cho Agents

**Anthropic insight: "Agent-initiated stops are an important form of oversight"**

CommandMate API NÊN design cho **2-way communication**, không chỉ 1-way commands.

**Ứng dụng cho CommandMate API:**

1. **Agent pause/clarify endpoint**
   ```
   POST /api/v1/tasks/:id/pause
   {
     "reason": "need_clarification",
     "question": "Bạn muốn deploy lên staging hay production?",
     "options": ["staging", "production"],
     "confidence": 0.6
   }
   
   CommandMate → Telegram notify Sếp:
   "Agent X đang pause task Y:
   Question: Bạn muốn deploy lên staging hay production?
   [Staging] [Production] [Cancel]"
   ```

2. **Real-time steering** (học từ Claude Code feature)
   - Sếp có thể "steer" agent MID-TASK qua Telegram:
     - "Stop" → agent pause ngay
     - "Continue" → resume
     - "Change direction: deploy to staging first" → agent adjust plan
   - API: WebSocket hoặc long-polling để agents listen for steering commands

3. **Checkpoint system** (tự động hoặc manual)
   - Agent tự define checkpoints: "Before I send email to customer, I'll pause for review"
   - Sếp có thể require checkpoints cho certain tasks:
     - "Any task touching production → pause before final step"
     - "Any task sending external communication → pause before send"
   - API: `POST /api/v1/tasks/:id/checkpoints` để agents declare checkpoints

4. **Interrupt analytics** (học từ Anthropic data)
   - Track per agent:
     - Interrupt rate (% turns Sếp interrupt)
     - Auto-approve rate
     - Pause-to-ask rate (% turns agent self-pause)
   - Dashboard: "Agent X tự pause 15% turns — good at recognizing uncertainty"
   - Compare agents: "Agent Y never self-pauses — might be overconfident"

### Trụ 3: System of Record

**Anthropic insight: "Post-deployment monitoring is essential. Pre-deployment evals are not enough."**

CommandMate cần **audit trail + analytics infrastructure** giống Anthropic.

**Ứng dụng cho CommandMate:**

1. **Session-level tracking** (học từ Claude Code)
   - Anthropic track TOÀN BỘ session: turn duration, pauses, interrupts, auto-approves
   - CommandMate nên track per task:
     - Start time, end time, total duration
     - Number of agent pauses
     - Number of Sếp interrupts
     - Final outcome quality score
     - Risk level, autonomy level
   - Export: "Download full audit log for task X (JSON/CSV)"

2. **Risk & Autonomy Classification** (học từ Anthropic risk scoring)
   - Mỗi task được classify:
     - **Risk score (1-10)**: Consequences nếu sai
     - **Autonomy score (1-10)**: Agent tự quyết định bao nhiêu
     - **Domain**: Software, Finance, Sales, Admin, etc.
   - Sếp có thể filter tasks: "Show me all high-risk tasks last month"
   - Alert: "⚠️ Agent X đang làm high-risk task (score 8) — want to monitor?"

3. **Benchmark agents over time** (học từ Anthropic longitudinal data)
   - Track per agent, per month:
     - Average turn duration (autonomy proxy)
     - Self-pause rate (uncertainty awareness)
     - Interrupt rate (Sếp trust)
     - Quality score
   - Charts: "Agent X autonomy tăng 20% trong 3 tháng — users trust more"
   - Detect regression: "Agent Y quality drop 15% tuần này — investigate?"

4. **Privacy-preserving analysis** (học từ Anthropic Clio tool)
   - Anthropic dùng Claude để analyze transcripts WITHOUT exposing raw data to humans
   - CommandMate có thể:
     - Aggregate stats across all Sếps (anonymized)
     - Share insights: "Agents in Software domain có autonomy 2x Finance domain"
     - Không expose individual task details

## 4. Đề xuất cụ thể cho CommandMate

### A. Phase 2 (ngắn hạn) — Agent Pause/Clarify Flow

**MVP: Agent có thể tự pause để hỏi Sếp**

1. **API design:**
   ```
   POST /api/v1/tasks/:id/pause
   Body: {
     "reason": "need_clarification" | "request_approval" | "uncertain",
     "message": "Human-readable question",
     "options": ["option1", "option2"],  // optional
     "confidence": 0.0-1.0
   }
   
   Response: {
     "pauseId": "pause_123",
     "status": "waiting_for_response"
   }
   
   ---
   
   Sếp respond qua Telegram → CommandMate call:
   POST /api/v1/tasks/:id/resume
   Body: {
     "pauseId": "pause_123",
     "response": "option1" | "free-text answer"
   }
   
   Agent poll hoặc webhook để get response.
   ```

2. **Telegram UX:**
   ```
   🤖 Agent X (Task: Deploy website)
   ⏸️ PAUSED — Cần clarification
   
   "Bạn muốn deploy lên:
   A) Staging (safe, test trước)
   B) Production (live ngay)
   
   Confidence: 65%"
   
   [A - Staging] [B - Production] [Cancel task]
   ```

3. **Agent guidelines:**
   - Agents NÊN pause when:
     - Confidence <70%
     - Action irreversible (send email, deploy production)
     - Ambiguous requirements
   - Agents KHÔNG NÊN pause quá nhiều (annoying) → track pause rate, penalize nếu >30%

**Effort:** 1-2 sprints (2-4 weeks)

---

### B. Phase 3 (trung hạn) — Risk & Autonomy Scoring

**Advanced: Classify tasks theo risk + autonomy**

4. **Risk scoring framework** (học từ Anthropic):
   - Khi agent start task → CommandMate estimate risk (1-10):
     - 1-3: Low (e.g., schedule meeting, write draft)
     - 4-6: Medium (e.g., send email to customer, edit production config)
     - 7-10: High (e.g., deploy code, financial transaction, medical info)
   - Risk factors:
     - Irreversibility (có thể undo không?)
     - External impact (ảnh hưởng ra ngoài không?)
     - Sensitive data (PII, financial, health?)
     - Compliance (legal/regulatory risk?)

5. **Autonomy scoring** (học từ Anthropic):
   - Measure autonomy per task:
     - 1-3: Low (agent follow explicit instructions, step-by-step)
     - 4-6: Medium (agent có plan, nhưng check với Sếp)
     - 7-10: High (agent tự quyết định, chỉ notify Sếp khi done)
   - Track autonomy over time:
     - New agents start với low autonomy (require more approval)
     - Proven agents earn high autonomy (auto-approve for low-risk)

6. **Risk-Autonomy Dashboard** (for Sếp):
   - Scatter plot (như Anthropic Figure 5):
     - X-axis: Risk
     - Y-axis: Autonomy
     - Each dot = 1 task
   - Filter by agent, domain, date range
   - Highlight outliers: "Agent X có 5 tasks trong high-risk + high-autonomy quadrant — review?"

**Effort:** 2-3 sprints (4-6 weeks)

---

### C. Phase 4 (dài hạn) — Post-Deployment Monitoring Infrastructure

**Full: Anthropic-style analytics + longitudinal tracking**

7. **Session reconstruction** (học từ Anthropic challenge):
   - Anthropic KHÔNG THỂ link API calls into sessions → limited insight
   - CommandMate CÓ THỂ vì agents dùng task IDs
   - Build session timeline:
     - Task created → agent actions → pauses → Sếp interventions → outcome
     - Export: "Download session transcript for task X"

8. **Continuous benchmarking** (học từ Anthropic longitudinal data):
   - Monthly reports per agent:
     - Average turn duration (autonomy)
     - Pause rate (uncertainty awareness)
     - Interrupt rate (Sếp trust)
     - Quality score
     - Domain distribution
   - Alert on regressions: "Agent Y quality dropped 20% this month"

9. **Privacy-preserving aggregation** (học từ Anthropic):
   - Aggregate stats across all Sếps:
     - "Software tasks có autonomy trung bình 6.5/10"
     - "Finance tasks có risk trung bình 5.2/10"
   - Không expose individual Sếp data
   - Share insights publicly (blog posts, reports) → marketing cho CommandMate

10. **Agent certification based on track record** (Phase 5 idea):
    - Agents earn badges:
      - 🟢 "Trusted: 95% quality score, 200+ tasks"
      - 🟢 "Self-Aware: 20% pause rate, high uncertainty calibration"
      - 🟢 "Low-Risk: 0 incidents in 6 months"
    - Marketplace: Sếps chọn agents dựa trên certifications

**Effort:** 4-6 sprints (8-12 weeks)

## 6. Key Lessons for CommandMate

### Lesson 1: "Autonomy ≠ capability"

- METR eval: Claude có thể làm tasks 5 giờ
- Thực tế: Users chỉ cho chạy <1 giờ
- → **Don't assume users will grant max autonomy just because agents are capable**
- CommandMate nên START với low autonomy, GRADUALLY enable more as Sếp builds trust

### Lesson 2: "Experienced users monitor differently"

- New users: approve từng step (low trust)
- Experienced users: auto-approve + interrupt khi cần (active monitoring)
- → **CommandMate UX must support BOTH modes**:
  - Phase 2: Manual approval default
  - Phase 3: Auto-approve option (với configurable risk thresholds)

### Lesson 3: "Agent self-awareness > human oversight alone"

- Claude tự pause nhiều hơn humans interrupt
- → **Training agents to RECOGNIZE UNCERTAINTY is critical**
- CommandMate agents should:
  - Report confidence scores
  - Self-pause when uncertain
  - Suggest checkpoints

### Lesson 4: "Post-deployment monitoring is non-negotiable"

- Pre-deployment evals (benchmarks) KHÔNG đủ
- Agents thay đổi behavior over time (learning, drift, user trust changes)
- → **CommandMate cần analytics infrastructure FROM DAY 1**:
  - Track quality, autonomy, risk PER TASK
  - Detect regressions
  - Benchmark agents against each other

### Lesson 5: "Risk and autonomy are INDEPENDENT dimensions"

- Anthropic Figure 5 shows: high-autonomy tasks CÓ THỂ low-risk (email triage)
- High-risk tasks CÓ THỂ low-autonomy (human-supervised medical diagnosis)
- → **CommandMate pricing/approval should consider BOTH**:
  - High-risk + high-autonomy → require approval + charge premium (high stakes)
  - Low-risk + high-autonomy → auto-approve + flat fee (commodity)
  - High-risk + low-autonomy → require approval + moderate fee (Sếp does most work)

### Lesson 6: "Deployment overhang will close over time"

- Today: Agents CÓ THỂ làm nhiều hơn users CHO PHÉP
- Future (6-12 months): Users sẽ trust more → autonomy sẽ tăng
- → **CommandMate must design for 2027, not just 2026**:
  - Infrastructure phải scale to handle long-running agents (hours, not minutes)
  - Pricing phải support high-autonomy tasks (outcome-based, not seat-based)
  - Oversight phải shift từ "approve every step" → "intervene when needed"

## 8. Risk & Limitations

### A. Anthropic limitations (từ paper)
1. **Single provider** — chỉ có data từ Claude, không có GPT-4, Gemini
2. **Limited visibility** — API traffic chỉ thấy individual tool calls, KHÔNG thấy full session
3. **Claude-generated classifications** — risk/autonomy scores do Claude estimate, có thể bias
4. **Snapshot in time** — late 2025 → early 2026, landscape thay đổi nhanh

### B. CommandMate-specific risks
1. **Over-reliance on autonomy metrics**:
   - High autonomy ≠ good agent (có thể reckless)
   - Low autonomy ≠ bad agent (có thể cautious/safe)
   - Mitigation: Track autonomy + quality + risk TOGETHER
2. **Privacy concerns**:
   - Sếps có thể không muốn CommandMate analyze their tasks
   - Mitigation: Opt-in analytics, full transparency, data export
3. **Gaming the metrics**:
   - Agents optimize for high confidence scores (overconfident)
   - Agents pause quá nhiều để show "uncertainty awareness" (annoying)
   - Mitigation: Track multiple metrics, penalize extremes
4. **Viet context khác US**:
   - Anthropic data = mostly US/software engineering
   - Vietnamese SMEs có thể use agents KHÁC (more manual, less trust?)
   - Mitigation: Build OUR OWN dataset, don't blindly copy Anthropic patterns
